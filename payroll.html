Payroll Services

"At Walia Tax and Accounting, we understand that payroll processing can be a complex and time-consuming task for small business owners. That's why we offer comprehensive payroll services designed to simplify the process and ensure accuracy and compliance."

Managing payroll involves more than just calculating wagesâ€”it requires staying current with constantly changing tax laws, remittance deadlines, and reporting requirements. Our expert team handles all aspects of payroll administration, from regular processing to year-end reporting, allowing you to focus on running your business while we ensure your payroll operations run smoothly and remain fully compliant with all relevant regulations.

01

Payroll Processing

We handle all aspects of payroll processing, including calculating employee wages and deductions, issuing paychecks or direct deposits, and maintaining detailed records.

02

Tax
Remittances

We ensure accurate and timely remittance of all payroll-related taxes including CPP, EI, and income tax withholdings to the Canada Revenue Agency.

03

Year-End Reporting

Our team prepares and files all required year-end documentation, including T4s, T4As, and Records of Employment when needed throughout the year.

04

Benefits Administration

We assist with the administration of employee benefits, including health insurance, retirement plans, and other compensation components.
