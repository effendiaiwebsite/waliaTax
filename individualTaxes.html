Personal Tax Services

"Our team of tax specialists is dedicated to ensuring that you receive the maximum tax refund possible, while also ensuring compliance with all applicable tax laws and regulations."

At Walia Tax, we understand that every individual has unique tax situations, which is why we take the time to thoroughly understand your financial situation, goals and objectives. Our tax preparation services are customized to meet your specific needs, ensuring that you receive the most accurate and comprehensive tax preparation possible. Whether you're a salaried employee, a retiree, a student, or someone with complex investment income, our specialists can navigate the complexities of the tax system on your behalf.

01

T1 Personal Tax Returns

Our comprehensive T1 filing service includes all necessary schedules and forms to ensure your personal income tax return is complete, accurate, and optimized for maximum refunds.

02

Employment
Expenses

We help you claim all eligible employment-related expenses including home office, vehicle expenses, supplies, and other costs that may qualify for tax deductions.

03

Investment Income

Our specialists properly report all types of investment income including dividends, capital gains, interest, foreign investments, and ensure proper tax treatment of each.

04

Student Tax Returns

We maximize benefits for students by properly claiming tuition credits, education amounts, textbook credits, and student loan interest to minimize tax liability.

How We Deliver

At Walia Tax, we use state-of-the-art technology and best practices to ensure that your tax returns are prepared accurately and efficiently. Our team has extensive experience in a wide range of tax areas.

Technology & Expertise
Year-Round Support
Special Situations

We leverage advanced tax preparation software and continuous professional education to stay current with all tax law changes. Our specialists have expertise in handling diverse personal tax situations ranging from simple returns to complex scenarios involving multiple income sources, investments, rental properties, and self-employment income. This combination of technology and expertise ensures accuracy and maximizes your refund potential.

Our relationship with you doesn't end after filing season. We provide year-round tax planning advice and support to help you make informed financial decisions throughout the year. This proactive approach allows you to take advantage of tax-saving opportunities before the year ends, potentially reducing your overall tax burden and improving your financial outcomes.

We have specialized experience handling unique tax situations such as new residents to Canada, individuals with foreign income, retirement planning, disability tax credits, and estate matters. Our team can also assist with prior-year tax filings, tax adjustments, and resolving issues with the CRA. Whatever your personal tax situation, our experienced team is equipped to provide the expertise you need.
