CRA Audit Support Services

"An audit from the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) can be a stressful and overwhelming experience for any taxpayer. At Walia Tax and Accounting, we offer professional CRA audit support to help you navigate the audit process and ensure that your rights as a taxpayer are protected."

Receiving a CRA audit notification doesn't have to be cause for panic. With our experienced team by your side, you can approach the audit process with confidence, knowing that experienced professionals are handling your case. We understand CRA procedures, documentation requirements, and taxpayer rights, allowing us to provide comprehensive support throughout every stage of the audit process, from initial notification to final resolution.

01

Pre-Audit Preparation

We help you prepare for the audit by reviewing your financial records, organizing required documentation, and developing a strategic approach to address potential areas of concern.

02

Audit
Representation

Our team represents you during the entire audit process, communicating with CRA auditors on your behalf, attending meetings, and advocating for your taxpayer rights.

03

Response Management

We prepare comprehensive responses to CRA information requests, ensuring all submissions are accurate, complete, and presented in a manner that supports your tax position.

04

Appeals Assistance

If audit results are unfavorable, we provide expert assistance with objections and appeals, developing strong arguments and compiling supporting evidence to challenge CRA assessments.

Expert CRA Audit Navigation

Our team has a thorough understanding of Canadian tax law and the audit process, and we are dedicated to protecting your rights as a taxpayer throughout the entire procedure.

Audit Risk Assessment:

We conduct a comprehensive review of your tax filings and financial records to identify potential audit triggers and areas of concern, allowing us to proactively address issues before they become problems during the audit.

Documentation Management:

We help organize, prepare, and present all necessary documentation in a manner that satisfies CRA requirements while supporting your tax positions, reducing the risk of additional assessments or penalties.

Why Choose Walia Tax for CRA Audit Support?

Choosing Walia Tax and Accounting for your CRA audit support means having an experienced team of tax professionals on your side who understand the nuances of CRA procedures and taxpayer rights.

Expertise & Experience
Stress Reduction
Financial Protection

Our team brings extensive experience in dealing with CRA audits across various tax areas including personal income tax, corporate tax, GST/HST, payroll, and trust filings. We understand CRA audit procedures, common focus areas, and the strategies that lead to successful outcomes. This expertise allows us to anticipate auditor concerns, prepare appropriate responses, and navigate complex tax regulations effectively. Our familiarity with CRA processes helps streamline the audit, potentially reducing its duration and scope while protecting your interests throughout.

CRA audits can be extremely stressful for taxpayers, especially those without professional representation. By engaging our services, you transfer the burden of audit management to experienced professionals who handle all communications, information gathering, and meetings with the CRA. This significantly reduces your personal stress while ensuring the audit is handled professionally. We explain the process in clear terms, keep you informed about progress, and help you understand what to expect at each stage, transforming an intimidating experience into a manageable process.

Our professional representation can provide significant financial protection during a CRA audit. We work diligently to defend legitimate deductions and credits, contest improper assessments, and ensure you don't pay more tax than legally required. If errors are discovered, we help minimize potential penalties and interest by demonstrating due diligence and good faith compliance efforts. Beyond the immediate audit, we identify opportunities to strengthen your tax filing practices, potentially reducing the risk of future audits and ensuring ongoing compliance with tax regulations.
